AMAN SANDUJA

He is Intrigued by Blockchain technology, design, photography, philosophy, fabulous food, and even better conversations. He in TBC is leading a team of researchers, working on blockchain 3.0, Hashgraph, quantum proof solutions, IOT & Blockchain Aman is seeking to be inspired, to envision the unlikely, to work hard for things that are worth it, and to be surrounded by those who bring out the best in me. He is an engineer and Masters by education. Prior to founding Towards Blockchain, Aman has worked with Piramal, Google, Ashoka University and startups. He is an active stock market and crypto trader. He has been following cryptocurrency from the early days of bitcoin and ethereum. Aman speaks extensively on Blockchain and Cryptocurrency across India. He is running a blockchain and cryptocurrency community and has plans to expand it internationally. He has recently spoken with CXOs of billion-dollar companies at Data Science forum. Aman strongly believes in co-creation, as the saying goes: two heads are better than one.
